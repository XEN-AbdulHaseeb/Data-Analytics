## Project Overview
This project analyzes the **Electric Vehicle (EV) market in the UAE** to evaluate market size, growth trends, consumer adoption, and strategic entry opportunities. The analysis is structured in a **consulting-style report** format, including market sizing, competitive landscape, infrastructure assessment, and opportunity gaps.

## Objectives
- Conduct **market sizing using TAM–SAM–SOM** frameworks.  
- Analyze **EV adoption trends and infrastructure growth**.  
- Assess **market drivers, challenges, and competitive dynamics**.  
- Provide actionable insights for **market entry strategy**.

## Key Metrics & Findings
- **Market Growth:** EV registrations increased from **8,500 in 2021 to 40,600 in 2025** (~48% CAGR).  
- **Consumer Interest:** 76% of potential buyers are open to EV adoption (26% plan to buy, 50% considering).  
- **Charging Infrastructure:** Public chargers grew from **200 → 1,270 (2020–2025)**; infrastructure pressure is **~32 EVs per charger**.  
- **Segment Opportunity:** Mid-segment dominated by Tesla Model 3, Model Y, and BYD Seal; potential for **affordable long-range EVs targeting first-time buyers**.
